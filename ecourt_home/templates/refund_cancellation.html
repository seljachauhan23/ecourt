eCourt - Privacy Policy

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Learn about our refund and cancellation policies to understand your rights and obligations when using our platform.

Introduction

At eCourt, we strive to ensure a seamless experience for all our users. This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains the terms and conditions under which refunds and cancellations may be requested and processed.

Refund Policy

Refunds are applicable under the following circumstances:

  • If a payment was made in error, and no services were rendered.
  • In case of a technical failure during payment processing resulting in duplicate transactions.
  • Other specific scenarios as per our platform's terms and conditions.

All refund requests must be submitted within 15 days of the transaction date. Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days.

Cancellation Policy

You may request a cancellation under the following conditions:

  • If the service has not yet been initiated.
  • For subscription services, cancellations are effective at the end of the billing cycle.
  • Any exceptions will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Cancellations must be requested through our support team, and you will receive a confirmation once the cancellation is processed.

Exceptions

Refunds and cancellations are not applicable in the following situations:

  • If the service has already been completed or delivered.
  • For non-refundable services explicitly mentioned during the payment process.
  • For delays or issues caused by factors beyond our control.
Need Help?

If you have any questions or concerns regarding refunds or cancellations, please reach out to our support team for assistance.
