Frequently Asked Questions

What is eCourt?

eCourt is an online platform designed to simplify court processes, enabling users to manage cases, hearings, and filings efficiently.

How can I register?

Register by filling out the signup form for your role (Citizen, Lawyer, Judge, or Admin) with the required details.

Can I file a case online?

Yes, citizens can file cases online by submitting the necessary documents through the eCourt platform.

Is my data secure?

eCourt ensures the safety of your data using encryption and secure systems to protect personal and legal information.

Can I track my case status?

Yes, users can track the progress of their cases and upcoming hearings through their personalized dashboard.
